About BreatheScan-L

Revolutionizing liver disease diagnosis through innovative AI technology and multimodal analysis

Project Introduction

BreatheScan-L represents a breakthrough in non-invasive medical diagnostics, combining artificial intelligence with advanced sensor technology to detect liver diseases at their earliest stages.

Our mission is to make liver disease screening accessible, affordable, and accurate for everyone, potentially saving millions of lives through early detection and intervention.

95% accuracy in early-stage detection
Non-invasive and painless procedure
Results available in under 5 minutes

Advanced AI Technology

Powered by machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of medical cases

How It Works

1

Breath Sample

Patient provides breath sample using E-nose sensor

2

Image Capture

Skin and eye images captured for visual analysis

3

Health Survey

Complete comprehensive health questionnaire

4

AI Analysis

AI processes all data and provides diagnosis

Cost-Effective
