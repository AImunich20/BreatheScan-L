Liver Health Knowledge Center

Comprehensive information about liver diseases, prevention strategies, and health maintenance

Common Liver Diseases

Hepatitis

Inflammation of the liver caused by viral infections, alcohol, or autoimmune conditions.

Fatigue and weakness
Jaundice (yellowing of skin/eyes)
Abdominal pain

Fatty Liver Disease

Accumulation of excess fat in liver cells, often related to obesity and diabetes.

Often asymptomatic early on
Upper right abdominal discomfort
Elevated liver enzymes

Liver Cancer

Malignant tumors in the liver, either primary or metastatic from other organs.

Unexplained weight loss
Abdominal swelling
Loss of appetite

Prevention & Care

Prevention Strategies

Maintain Healthy Weight

Regular exercise and balanced diet to prevent fatty liver disease

Limit Alcohol Consumption

Moderate or eliminate alcohol intake to protect liver function

Get Vaccinated

Hepatitis A and B vaccines provide effective protection

Regular Screening

Early detection through regular health checkups and testing
